About Pac-Man

History

Pac-Man is a video game created by Namco and first released in Japan in 1980.

Gameplay

  • The player controls Pac-Man, a yellow circle with a mouth, through a maze.
  • Pac-Man must eat all the dots in the maze while avoiding four ghosts.
  • If Pac-Man eats a special "power pellet", he can eat the ghosts.

Legacy

  1. Pac-Man is often cited as one of the greatest video games of all time.
  2. The game has inspired numerous spin-offs, sequels, and merchandise.
  3. The character of Pac-Man has become an icon of video game culture and is recognizable around the world.
